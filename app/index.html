Q&A
Where and when?

We will start at 5pm and will be done at about 11pm after which you will have the opportunity to network and socialize. We will invite you to the Burda Bootcamp, Arabellastrasse 23, 2nd floor, Munich.

When does the application period end?

You can apply until August, 28st. We will let you know by September 4th, if you have been selected, so that you have enough time to prepare your pitch.

What type of startups are you looking for?

Our main focus are the following areas: Food, Fashion, Beauty, Home & Garden, News, Entertainment, Technology, Advertising, Mobile & Social. However, we are not limited to these, so feel free to apply, even if you don’t find yourself in one of those categories.

What do we need to prepare?

Just fill out the questionnaire and we will get back to you if we need more information. Selected startups will then have to prepare a 3-min pitchdeck and a small booth for the evening September 13th, 2017.

Do we get any funding?

We do not give financial support, but we help startups to develop their business further by supporting them with free coworking space, mentoring as well as access to the Burda network.

What happens after the 3-months?

Together with your team we will see how far you have developed your business and come up with a follow-up plan. We will discuss options together with you during your 3-months at the Burda Bootcamp.

How do you judge the pitches?

You have to be awesome!
Apart from that, we will challenge your business model and market potential. We will also pay close attention to the founding team.

Startup Fair before pitches

If you have been chosen to pitch, you should prepare a stand for our Startup Fair where you may present your startup to the jury and guests before and after the pitches and also during the break when the jury is discussing their decision. You will have a table available (Size: 1,40m/0,60m) and can get creative with the set up!

Further questions?

Please feel free to contact us anytime! startups@burdabootcamp.de

Diese Art von Cookies bleibt dauerhaft (auch nach Beendigung des Browsers) bestehen und speichert bspw. Zugangskennungen.

  • Drittanbieter-Cookies:
